Empirical Study on Agentic Browsing Heuristics

Authors: K. Ustad, et al. | Date: March 2026

Abstract

This paper presents a detailed investigation into the retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) capabilities of modern large language models. We focus specifically on how the 'browsing' tool interacts with third-party CSS assets and DOM-based telemetry. By analyzing the latency between initial GET requests and final token output, we can map the internal architecture of the agent's rendering engine.
